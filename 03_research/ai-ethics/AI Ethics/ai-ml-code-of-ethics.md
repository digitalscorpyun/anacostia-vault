# AI/ML Code of Ethics

The **AI/ML Code of Ethics** outlines the guiding principles for ethical AI development within the Anacostia Vault. This document serves as the foundation for ensuring that all AI projects reflect values of fairness, accountability, and social justice.

---

## Core Principles

- **Fairness & Non-Discrimination:**  
  Ensure that all AI systems treat individuals equitably and do not reinforce historical or systemic biases. Use fairness metrics (e.g., demographic parity, equalized odds) as benchmarks for evaluation.

- **Transparency & Accountability:**  
  Maintain clarity in the design and implementation of algorithms. Every model should be accompanied by documentation that details data sources, methodologies, and potential biases. Establish feedback loops for affected communities to raise concerns.

- **Decolonial Approach:**  
  Integrate Africana epistemologies and decolonial perspectives to challenge traditional Eurocentric paradigms in AI. Focus on how systemic power imbalances are reflected—and can be remedied—through technological interventions.

- **Privacy & Security:**  
  Safeguard personal data and ensure that AI systems respect user privacy. Prioritize robust data protection measures and ethical data governance practices.

- **Inclusivity in Design:**  
  Involve diverse stakeholders in the AI development process to broaden perspectives and preempt culturally insensitive outputs. Seek partnerships with community organizations and experts from underrepresented backgrounds.

---

## Implementation Guidelines

1. **Ethical Audits:**  
   - Regularly perform bias detection and fairness audits using tools like IBM AI Fairness 360 and Fairlearn.
   - Document and address any identified issues promptly.

2. **Transparent Reporting:**  
   - Maintain open logs and reports for AI system performance and decisions.
   - Publish summaries that detail both successes and areas for improvement.

3. **Community Engagement:**  
   - Create channels for stakeholders to provide feedback on AI systems.
   - Collaborate with interdisciplinary teams to assess societal impact.

4. **Continuous Learning:**  
   - Stay updated with emerging research and best practices in AI ethics.
   - Regularly review and refine this code to reflect new insights and technological advancements.

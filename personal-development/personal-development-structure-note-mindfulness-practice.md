---

## 🧘 **Core Mindfulness Techniques**

- **Mindful Breathing** – Focused awareness on the breath as an anchor to the present moment.
    - **Body Scan Meditation** – Progressive attention to physical sensations for relaxation and awareness.
- **Loving-Kindness Meditation (Metta)** – Cultivating compassion for oneself and others.
- **Mindful Walking** – Engaging fully with the act of walking to ground oneself in the present.
- **Journaling & Reflection** – Documenting thoughts, feelings, and experiences for self-awareness.

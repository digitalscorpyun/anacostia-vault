#### **Cajun Chicken Recipe**

**Ingredients:**

- 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
    
- 2 tbsp olive oil
    
- 1 tbsp Cajun seasoning (or more to taste)
    
- 1 tsp paprika
    
- ½ tsp garlic powder
    
- ½ tsp onion powder
    
- Salt & pepper to taste
    
- Optional: splash of lemon juice, cayenne for heat
    

**Instructions:**

1. **Prep the chicken**: Pat dry, pound if needed for even thickness.
    
2. **Mix spices**: Combine Cajun seasoning, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper.
    
3. **Rub & marinate**: Coat chicken with olive oil, then rub in seasoning. Let sit for at least 30 mins (overnight if you're fancy).
    
4. **Sear**: Heat skillet (cast iron if you're goin' traditional), medium-high heat. Cook chicken ~6 minutes per side until beautifully blackened and cooked through.
    
5. **Rest**: Let it sit 5 minutes before slicing. Optional: squeeze lemon over the top for that _zing_.
    

**Serve with**:

- Dirty rice
    
- Cornbread
    
- Collard greens
    
- Sweet tea (if you know, you know)

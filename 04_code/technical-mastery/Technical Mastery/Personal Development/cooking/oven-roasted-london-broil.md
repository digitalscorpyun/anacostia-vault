## 🔥 **Cajun-Creole Oven-Roasted London Broil** (Feeds 4)

### 🛒 Ingredients

#### 🥩 For the Meat:

- 2.33 lbs London Broil (top round or flank)
    
- 2 tbsp olive oil
    
- 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
    
- 2 tsp apple cider vinegar or lemon juice (for tenderizing)
    
- 2 tsp Creole mustard (or Dijon if that’s what you got)
    

#### 🌶️ Cajun Spice Rub (mix it yourself):

- 1 tbsp smoked paprika
    
- 1 tsp cayenne (adjust heat level to your soul)
    
- 1 tsp black pepper
    
- 1 tsp white pepper (optional, but nice if you got it)
    
- 1 tsp garlic powder
    
- 1 tsp onion powder
    
- 1 tsp dried thyme
    
- 1 tsp dried oregano
    
- ½ tsp kosher salt (or more to taste)
    

#### 🧄 Creole Garlic Herb Butter:

- 3 tbsp butter, melted
    
- 2 garlic cloves, finely minced
    
- 1 tsp chopped parsley or green onion
    
- A splash of lemon juice
    

---

## 🥄 Instructions

### 🔪 Step 1: Prep & Marinate (30 min to overnight)

1. **Pat the London Broil dry**.
    
2. Rub it down with:
    
    - Olive oil
        
    - Worcestershire sauce
        
    - Vinegar or lemon juice
        
    - Mustard
        
3. Mix the **Cajun Spice Rub**, and coat both sides of the meat generously.
    
4. Wrap it up or place it in a covered dish. Let it **marinate for 30 mins minimum**, but overnight is 🔥 if you have time.
    

---

### 🔥 Step 2: Sear for the Crust (Optional but Worth It)

If you have a cast iron or oven-safe skillet:

1. Preheat your oven to **425°F (218°C)**.
    
2. Heat a skillet with a little oil on high.
    
3. Sear the London Broil for **2–3 minutes per side** until a crust forms.
    

---

### 🍖 Step 3: Roast in Oven

1. Transfer meat (skillet or baking dish) into the preheated oven.
    
2. Roast for:
    
    - **12–14 minutes for medium rare**
        
    - **15–18 minutes for medium**
        
    - Internal temp target: **130°F–135°F** for medium rare (use a meat thermometer if possible)
        
3. Once done, remove and let it **rest for 10 minutes** before slicing.
    

---

### 🧈 Step 4: Finish with Creole Butter

While it rests, pour over your garlic herb butter. Watch it melt in like your ancestors whisperin' blessings. 😤

---

### 🍽 Serving Suggestions

Cut the roast **across the grain**, thinly sliced. Pair with:

- **Dirty rice** or **Red beans & rice**
    
- **Maque choux** (Creole corn sauté)
    
- **Roasted okra** or **garlic green beans**
    
- Hot sauce or remoulade on the side
    

---

### 💡 Chef’s Notes:

- Got bell peppers and onions? Roast them in the same pan. They'll soak up that spicy goodness.
    
- Want to go hard? Add a dash of bourbon or hot honey to your Creole butter.

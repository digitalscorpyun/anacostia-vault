## **Daily Exercises**  
**Commit to 7 minutes a day until May 1st** for better strength, flexibility, and endurance!  

---

### **Monday Routine**
1. **Chair Dips** – 1 minute  
   [How To: Chair Dips](https://www.healthline.com/health/fitness-exercise/chair-dips)  
2. **Seated Lean Backs** – 2 minutes  
   [How To: Seated Lean Backs](https://www.shape.com/exercises/seated-lean-back)  
3. **Chair Crunch** – 4 minutes  
   [How To: Chair Crunch](https://www.livestrong.com/article/351672-how-to-do-chair-crunches/)  

---

### **Wednesday Routine**
1. **Alternating Cross Crunch** – 1 minute  
   [How To: Alternating Cross Crunch](https://www.verywellfit.com/cross-body-crunch-1230835)  
2. **Incline Push-Ups** – 2 minutes  
   [How To: Incline Push-Ups](https://www.verywellfit.com/incline-push-up-exercise-3120373)  
3. **Mountain Climbers** – 2 minutes  
   [How To: Mountain Climbers](https://www.healthline.com/health/mountain-climbers-exercise)  
4. **Step Up on a Chair** – 2 minutes  
   [How To: Step Ups](https://www.masterclass.com/articles/how-to-step-ups)  

---

### **Friday Routine**
1. **Chair Crunch** – 3 minutes  
   [How To: Chair Crunch](https://www.livestrong.com/article/351672-how-to-do-chair-crunches/)  
2. **Back Push-Ups** – 2 minutes  
   [How To: Back Push-Ups](https://www.nhs.uk/fitness/exercise-guide/push-ups-back/)  
3. **Twisting on a Chair** – 1 minute  
   [How To: Chair Twists](https://www.healthline.com/health/fitness-exercise/seated-spinal-twist)  
4. **Push-Ups on a Chair** – 1 minute  
   [How To: Push-Ups on a Chair](https://www.verywellfit.com/incline-push-up-exercise-3120373)  

---

### **Tips for Success**
- Maintain proper form to avoid injuries.
- Adjust intensity as needed—shorten intervals if you’re a beginner or increase the time as you progress.
- Stay hydrated and stretch before starting your routine.

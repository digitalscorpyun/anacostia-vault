### **Creole Smothered Chicken with Cajun Dirty Rice (2 Servings)**

#### **Ingredients & Measurements:**

#### **For the Smothered Chicken:**

✔ **Chicken leg meat** – **½ lb (about 1 thigh or equivalent in boneless meat)**  
✔ **Garlic cloves** – **2 cloves, minced**  
✔ **Brown onion** – **½ medium, diced**  
✔ **Green bell pepper** – **½ medium, diced**  
✔ **Celery** – **1 rib, diced** _(already chopped)_  
✔ **Carrots** – **½ medium carrot, sliced**  
✔ **Hot and mild smoked sausage** – **¼ cup, diced**  
✔ **Chicken broth** – **1 ¼ cups** (or water if substituting)  
✔ **Ketchup** – **½ teaspoon** _(just a small hint for depth)_  
✔ **Seasonings:**

- **Paprika** – **½ teaspoon**
- **Thyme** – **¼ teaspoon**
- **Black pepper** – **¼ teaspoon**
- **Cayenne** – **⅛ teaspoon** (adjust to taste)
- **Salt** – **½ teaspoon**

#### **For the Dirty Rice:**

✔ **Rice** – **½ cup (uncooked)**  
✔ **Chicken broth** – **1 cup** _(to cook rice with more flavor)_  
✔ **Garlic cloves** – **1 clove, minced**  
✔ **Brown onion** – **¼ medium, diced**  
✔ **Green onions** – **1 stalk, sliced (for garnish and flavor boost)**  
✔ **Pepper jack cheese** – **1 tbsp, grated (optional garnish)**

### **Suggested Meal: Creole Smothered Chicken with Cajun Dirty Rice**

Since you’ve already chopped **celery, bell pepper, and onions** (the Holy Trinity of Cajun cooking!), let’s use that to make something **soulful and rich**.

#### **What You'll Need from Your List:**

✔ Chicken leg meat  
✔ Green onions  
✔ Garlic cloves  
✔ Brown onions  
✔ Green bell pepper  
✔ Celery (you already chopped this)  
✔ Carrots  
✔ Hot and mild smoked sausage  
✔ Rice  
✔ Chicken broth (if you have it, or use water)  
✔ Ketchup (small amount)  
✔ Pepper jack cheese (for garnish, optional)  
✔ Seasonings (paprika, thyme, black pepper, cayenne, salt)

#### **How to Make It:**

1. **Season the Chicken:**
    
    - Generously coat the chicken leg meat with **salt, black pepper, cayenne, paprika, thyme**, and a small splash of **Cholula hot sauce** for depth.
2. **Brown the Chicken:**
    
    - Heat some oil in a deep skillet, **sear the chicken** until golden brown on both sides, then remove.
3. **Make the Holy Trinity Base:**
    
    - In the same pan, **sauté your chopped onions, bell peppers, celery, and garlic** until softened.
4. **Add the Sausage & Carrots:**
    
    - Dice and toss in **hot and mild smoked sausage** and sliced **carrots** for more flavor.
5. **Deglaze & Simmer:**
    
    - Stir in **a teaspoon of ketchup** (for a hint of tang) and enough **chicken broth** to cover the meat.
    - Add the browned chicken back in, cover, and **let it simmer for 45 minutes**.
6. **Cook the Rice Dirty Style:**
    
    - While the chicken simmers, cook the **rice** separately.
    - Once the rice is done, mix in **some of the smothered gravy and sausage bits** to give it that dirty rice flavor.
7. **Serve:**
    
    - Plate the chicken **over the Cajun dirty rice**.
    - Sprinkle **chopped green onions** and **grated pepper jack cheese** on top if you want a little extra punch.

🔥 **Optional Additions:**

- A side of **tortillas** if you want to make Cajun tacos.
- A simple salad with **romaine lettuce, Roma tomatoes, and cucumbers**.
- Finish with **red seedless grapes or red apples** for a fresh balance.

This dish **lets the Cajun flavors shine**, gives you **comfort and spice**, and works with **ingredients you already have on hand**. Plus, that **smoked sausage is going to make that sauce incredible**.

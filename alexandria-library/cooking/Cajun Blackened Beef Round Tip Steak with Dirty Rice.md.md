### **Cajun Blackened Beef Round Tip Steak with Dirty Rice**

#### **Ingredients:**

- **For the Steak:**
    
    - 1.19 lbs beef round tip steak
    - 2 tbsp Cajun seasoning (store-bought or homemade*)
    - 1 tbsp olive oil
    - 1 tbsp butter
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
    - Juice of 1/2 lemon
- **For the Dirty Rice:**
    
    - 1 cup long-grain white rice
    - 2 cups beef broth (or water with bouillon)
    - 1/2 lb ground beef or chopped liver (optional, for authentic dirty rice)
    - 1/2 cup chopped onion (part of the Holy Trinity)
    - 1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper (Holy Trinity)
    - 1/2 cup chopped celery (Holy Trinity)
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 1 tbsp Cajun seasoning
    - 1 tsp thyme
    - 1/2 tsp paprika
    - 2 green onions, chopped (for garnish)
    - Salt & black pepper to taste

#### **Instructions:**

1. **Prepare the Steak:**
    
    - Pat the beef dry with paper towels.
    - Rub the steak generously with Cajun seasoning, pressing it into the meat.
    - Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat until it starts to shimmer.
    - Add the steak and sear for 3–4 minutes per side for medium-rare (adjust based on thickness).
    - During the last minute, add butter, minced garlic, Worcestershire sauce, and lemon juice. Baste the steak with the melted butter mixture.
    - Remove from heat, cover loosely with foil, and let it rest for 5–10 minutes.
2. **Cook the Dirty Rice:**
    
    - In another skillet, cook the ground beef (if using) until browned. Drain excess fat.
    - Add the chopped onion, bell pepper, celery, and garlic. Sauté until softened, about 5 minutes.
    - Stir in Cajun seasoning, thyme, and paprika.
    - Add rice, stirring to coat with the spices and veggies.
    - Pour in the beef broth, bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer.
    - Cover and cook for 18–20 minutes until the rice is tender and the liquid is absorbed.
    - Fluff with a fork, adjust seasoning with salt and pepper, and garnish with green onions.
3. **Serve:**
    
    - Slice the rested steak thinly against the grain.
    - Serve over a bed of dirty rice, spooning any leftover pan juices on top.

#### _Homemade Cajun Seasoning (optional):_

- 1 tbsp paprika
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp onion powder
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 1 tsp dried thyme
- 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1/2 tsp white pepper
- 1/2 tsp salt

---

Enjoy this hearty, flavor-packed Cajun dish! Perfect with a side of cornbread or sautéed greens if you have them.

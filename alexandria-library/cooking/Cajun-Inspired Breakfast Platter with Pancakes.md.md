### 🥞 **Cajun-Inspired Breakfast Platter with Pancakes** 🍳

#### **Ingredients:**

- **For the Pancakes:**
    
    - Pancake mix (or from scratch: flour, baking powder, milk, egg)
    - 1 ripe **banana**, sliced
    - 1 tsp cinnamon (optional)
    - Butter for cooking
- **For the Savory Side:**
    
    - 2 **potatoes**, diced
    - 4 slices of **bacon**, chopped
    - 4 oz **smoked sausage**, sliced
    - 2 **eggs**
    - The **Holy Trinity** (1/4 cup each of diced **onion**, **bell pepper**, and **celery**)
    - Salt, pepper, and a pinch of Cajun seasoning (optional)
    - Oil or bacon fat for frying

---

#### **Instructions:**

1. **Pancakes with Bananas:**
    
    - Prepare pancake batter as directed.
    - Add a pinch of cinnamon (optional) and gently fold in half of the banana slices.
    - Cook pancakes on a buttered skillet until golden brown on both sides.
    - Top with remaining banana slices when serving.
2. **Savory Skillet Hash:**
    
    - In a large skillet, cook the chopped bacon until crispy. Remove and set aside, leaving the fat in the pan.
    - Add the smoked sausage slices to the pan and brown them. Remove and set aside with the bacon.
    - In the same pan, sauté the diced potatoes until golden and tender, adding a bit of oil if needed.
    - Add the **Holy Trinity** (onion, bell pepper, celery) and cook until softened.
    - Season with salt, pepper, and Cajun seasoning.
    - Stir the bacon and sausage back into the skillet.
3. **Eggs Your Way:**
    
    - In a separate pan, cook the eggs to your preference (scrambled, sunny-side up, or over-easy).

---

#### **Serving:**

Plate the pancakes with banana slices on the side, the savory hash next to them, and the eggs on top or on the side. Drizzle with syrup if you like a sweet-and-savory combo.

Enjoy your hearty, flavorful breakfast! 🍴

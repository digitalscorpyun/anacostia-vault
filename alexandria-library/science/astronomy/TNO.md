A **trans-Neptunian object** (**TNO**), also written **transneptunian object**,[[1]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Trans-Neptunian_object#cite_note-1) is any [minor planet](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Minor_planet "Minor planet") in the [Solar System](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Solar_System "Solar System") that [orbits](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Orbit "Orbit") the [Sun](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sun "Sun") at a greater average distance than [Neptune](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Neptune "Neptune"), which has an orbital [semi-major axis](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Semi-major_axis "Semi-major axis") of 30.1 [astronomical units](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Astronomical_unit "Astronomical unit") (AU).

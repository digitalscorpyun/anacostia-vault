## 📌 **Overview**

**Calculus** is the branch of mathematics that studies **change and motion** through **derivatives and integrals**. It is the foundation of **physics, engineering, economics, data science, and AI**.

It is divided into two main branches:  
1️⃣ **Differential Calculus** – Studying rates of change (slopes, derivatives).  
2️⃣ **Integral Calculus** – Studying accumulation (areas, integrals).

## 🏷️ **Key Topics in Calculus**

### **1️⃣ Limits & Continuity**

- **Definition of a Limit** – Approaching values as x → a.
- **One-Sided & Two-Sided Limits** – Left-hand and right-hand limits.
- **Continuity** – Checking if a function is continuous at a point.

### **2️⃣ Derivatives & Differential Calculus**

- **Definition of a Derivative** – Instantaneous rate of change.
- **Basic Differentiation Rules** – Power, product, quotient, chain rule.
- **Applications of Derivatives** – Tangent lines, velocity, acceleration, optimization.

### **3️⃣ Integrals & Integral Calculus**

- **Definite & Indefinite Integrals** – Finding areas under curves.
- **Fundamental Theorem of Calculus** – Relationship between differentiation and integration.
- **Techniques of Integration** – Substitution, integration by parts, partial fractions.

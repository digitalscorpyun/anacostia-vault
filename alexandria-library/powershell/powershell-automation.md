## 📜 **Historical Context**

- **Origins**: PowerShell debuted in 2006 as a .NET-based successor to CMD, designed by Jeffrey Snover.  
- **Key Milestones**: Open-sourced in 2016 (PowerShell Core), it now runs cross-platform, amplifying its reach.  
- **Vault Relevance**: Automates vault upkeep (e.g., [[validate_backlinks_script]]) and secures [[cybersecurity-activist-tools]].

- **Purpose**: Pre-built commands (e.g., `Get-Process`, `Set-Item`)—e.g., `Get-ChildItem -Path "vault"` lists files.

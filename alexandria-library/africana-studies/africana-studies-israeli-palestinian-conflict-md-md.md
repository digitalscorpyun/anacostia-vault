# 🏛 **Africana Studies - Israeli-Palestinian Conflict**

> _"We know too well that our freedom is incomplete without the freedom of the Palestinians." — Nelson Mandela_

---

## 📌 **Overview**

The **Israeli-Palestinian conflict** is a **decades-long struggle** over land, national identity, and sovereignty between **Israelis and Palestinians**. Rooted in **colonial history, displacement, and competing nationalisms**, the conflict has shaped global politics, affected millions of lives, and remains one of the most pressing international issues today.

For many in the **Africana world**, the Palestinian struggle resonates with the **anti-colonial and civil rights movements** that shaped the Black liberation struggle. Leaders like **Malcolm X, Nelson Mandela, and the Black Panther Party** have drawn parallels between **Zionism, apartheid, and racial oppression**.

---

## 🏷️ **Key Themes**

- **Settler-Colonialism & Displacement** – Palestinian lands systematically taken since 1948.
- **Apartheid & Segregation** – Israel’s policies compared to **South African apartheid**.
- **Resistance & Liberation** – Palestinian struggle linked to **global Black liberation movements**.
- **U.S. & Western Support for Israel** – A key factor in the power imbalance.
- **Africana Solidarity** – Pan-African and Black radical movements support **Palestinian self-determination**.

## 📜 **Historical Timeline**

📍 **1917** – **Balfour Declaration** supports a Jewish homeland in Palestine.  
📍 **1947-1948** – **UN Partition Plan** & establishment of **Israel**; mass displacement of **750,000+ Palestinians** (Nakba).  
📍 **1967** – **Six-Day War** leads to Israeli occupation of **West Bank, Gaza, and East Jerusalem**.  
📍 **1987-1993** – **First Intifada** (uprising) against Israeli military rule.  
📍 **1993-2000** – **Oslo Accords** signed but fail to establish a Palestinian state.  
📍 **2000-2005** – **Second Intifada** erupts over failed peace talks & Israeli expansion.  
📍 **2006-Present** – **Gaza under blockade**, continuous settler expansion, periodic wars.

## ⚖️ **Key Issues & Points of Contention**

### **1️⃣ Israeli Settler-Colonialism & Land Theft**

- **Illegal settlements** continue to expand in the **West Bank & East Jerusalem**.
- **Land seizures and home demolitions** displace Palestinian families.
- **Apartheid roads & segregated infrastructure** mirror South African apartheid.

### **2️⃣ Human Rights Violations & Apartheid**

- **Israel controls movement, economy, and daily life** in Palestinian territories.
- **Mass incarceration** of Palestinian activists (modeled after U.S. Black incarceration rates).
- **Gaza under blockade**, described as an **open-air prison** by the UN.

- **BLM & Pan-African movements call for Palestinian self-determination**.

- **The conflict shapes global politics, African relations with Israel, and U.S. foreign policy.**
- **Palestinian activism is increasingly intertwined with global racial justice movements.**
- **The Boycott, Divestment, Sanctions (BDS) movement mirrors anti-apartheid campaigns.**

## ⚠️ **Challenges & Continued Struggles**

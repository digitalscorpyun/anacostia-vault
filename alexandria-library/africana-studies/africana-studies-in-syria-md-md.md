# 🏛 **Africana Studies - Palestinian Militias in Syria**

> _"Palestinian resistance takes many forms, but the struggle for liberation remains constant."_

---

## 📌 **Overview**

Palestinian militias in Syria have played a **complex role in Middle Eastern conflicts**, particularly during the **Syrian Civil War (2011–2024)**. While originally founded to support **Palestinian self-determination**, many of these groups aligned themselves with the **Ba'athist government of Bashar al-Assad**, fighting against **Syrian opposition forces and extremist groups**.

Some militias, such as **Fatah al-Intifada** and the **Free Palestine Movement (FPM)**, became **pro-Assad paramilitary groups**, while others, like the **Palestine Liberation Army (PLA)**, maintained a more **neutral or fractured stance**. After the **fall of the Assad regime in 2024**, these militias faced **disarmament orders** from the new Syrian government.

## 📜 **Historical Context & Role in the Syrian Civil War**

📍 **Pre-2011** – Palestinian militias in Syria existed primarily as **self-defense forces for refugee camps**, with some ties to the **PLO, Ba'athists, or Islamist groups**.  
📍 **2011-2024** – The **Syrian Civil War** caused a major split among Palestinian factions; some militias, like **Liwa al-Quds and FPM**, **sided with Assad**, while others **attempted neutrality**.  
📍 **2018** – Palestinian factions played a key role in battles such as the **Southern Damascus offensives**, fighting both **ISIS and Syrian opposition forces**.  
📍 **2024** – After **the fall of Assad**, the **Syrian transitional government ordered the disarmament** of all Palestinian militias.

---

## ⚖️ **Political & Military Influence**

- Palestinian militias **held strategic areas** in **Damascus, Aleppo, and Deir ez-Zor**.
- Some factions, like **Liwa al-Quds**, received **Iranian support**, influencing regional geopolitics.
- The **Yarmouk Camp siege (2012–2015)** was one of the most significant events, with militias fighting against **both Syrian rebels and ISIS**.

## 🌟 **Impact & Legacy**

- Palestinian militias in Syria shaped **both the Palestinian resistance and Syrian war dynamics**.
- The forced **disarmament of militias in 2024** left uncertainty for **Palestinian communities in Syria**.
- Some former fighters joined **Lebanese and Iranian-backed groups**, while others **returned to political activism**.

## ⚠️ **Controversies & Allegations**

- **Human Rights Abuses:** Some militias were accused of **war crimes** during the **Siege of Yarmouk Camp**.
- **Forced Displacement:** Accusations of **land seizures** in **Palestinian refugee camps**.
- **Criminal Activities:** Allegations of **weapon smuggling and corruption** post-2024.

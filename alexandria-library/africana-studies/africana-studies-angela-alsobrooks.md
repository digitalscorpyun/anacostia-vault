### **📢 Title:** Angela Alsobrooks

### **📚 Type:** Person

---
### **🌟 Overview:**

Angela Alsobrooks is a dynamic American politician and attorney, currently serving as the junior U.S. Senator from Maryland 🌿. She made history as one of the **first two Black women** to serve simultaneously in the U.S. Senate alongside Lisa Blunt Rochester 💪🏾. As the **first woman elected** as County Executive for Prince George’s County, Maryland, her leadership has paved the way for transformative change 🚀.

---

### **📌 Key Details:**

- **👤 Full Name:** Angela Deneece Alsobrooks
- **🎂 Date of Birth:** February 23, 1971
- **🏛️ Political Affiliation:** Democratic Party 🌐
- **🏅 Historical Significance:**
    - First woman County Executive of Prince George’s County 👩🏾‍⚖️
    - Among the first Black women to serve concurrently in the U.S. Senate ✊🏾
- **🔑 Key Roles:**
    - U.S. Senator from Maryland (2025–present) 🇺🇸
    - County Executive, Prince George’s County (2018–2025) 🌿
    - State’s Attorney for Prince George’s County (2011–2018) ⚖️
- **🌍 Policy Focus:** Justice reform, education equity 📚, healthcare access 🏥, economic growth 📈, environmental justice 🌱

---

### **🗓️ Timeline:**

- **1971:** Born in Suitland, Maryland 🏡
- **1993:** Graduated from Duke University 🎓
- **1996:** Earned J.D. from University of Maryland School of Law 📜
- **2010:** Elected as State’s Attorney ⚖️
- **2018:** Made history as first woman County Executive 👩🏾‍💼
- **2024:** Elected to the U.S. Senate, breaking barriers 🚀
- **2025:** Begins Senate term, shaping national policies 🇺🇸

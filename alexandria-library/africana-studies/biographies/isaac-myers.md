## 📜 **The 👑 Royal Purple Triumph – Isaac Myers**

1. **Early Life and Training** 🟣  
   Born free in Baltimore on January 13, 1835, Myers learned to read via a private school under Rev. John Fortie, as Maryland denied Black education—[[africana-studies-educational-equity]]. Apprenticed at 16 to caulker James Jackson, he mastered the trade by 20—[[africana-studies-black-technology-innovators]]. This 👑 royal purple start shaped his rise.

2. **Response to Exclusion** 🟣  
   Post-Civil War, white caulkers’ strikes in 1865 forced 1,000 Black workers, including Myers, out of jobs—[[africana-studies-systemic-racism]]. He founded the Chesapeake Marine Railway and Dry Dock Company in 1866, employing 300 Black workers—[[africana-studies-racial-wealth-gap]]. This 🏰 royal purple act defied segregation.

3. **Labor Leadership** 🟣

4. **Legacy and Challenges** 🟣

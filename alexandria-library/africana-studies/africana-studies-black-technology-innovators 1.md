# Africana Studies - Black Technology Innovators

> _"Black innovators have shaped the technological landscape, from early engineering breakthroughs to modern advancements in AI, space exploration, and computing."_

## 📌 Overview

The contributions of Black innovators to technology and science are vast and often overlooked. From early pioneers in engineering to leaders in modern computing, artificial intelligence, and space exploration, Black technologists have played a crucial role in shaping the world we live in today.

## ⚙️ Notable Black Innovators in Technology

### **Early Pioneers & Engineers**

- **Granville T. Woods** – Inventor of the multiplex telegraph and railway safety systems.
- **Lewis Latimer** – Improved carbon filament for light bulbs and contributed to early electrical systems.
- **Garrett Morgan** – Invented the three-position traffic signal and the gas mask.
- **Madam C.J. Walker** – Created a successful beauty empire, pioneering Black entrepreneurship and industrialization.

### **Modern Computing & Cybersecurity**

- **Mark Dean** – Co-inventor of the color PC monitor and key figure in IBM’s early computers.
- **Kimberly Bryant** – Founder of Black Girls Code, expanding tech education for young Black women.
- **Timnit Gebru** – Researcher in AI ethics, known for exposing bias in machine learning systems.
- **Lisa Gelobter** – Helped develop the animation technology behind GIFs.

### **AI, Space Exploration & Science**

- **Katherine Johnson** – NASA mathematician whose calculations enabled the first American space missions.
- **Mae Jemison** – First Black woman in space and advocate for STEM education.
- **Aprille Ericsson** – Aerospace engineer at NASA, working on satellite and space missions.
- **Dr. Lonnie Johnson** – NASA engineer and inventor of the Super Soaker water gun, with contributions to energy technology.

### **Biotechnology & Medical Innovations**

- **Patricia Bath** – Invented the Laserphaco Probe for cataract surgery.
- **George Washington Carver** – Agricultural scientist who revolutionized crop rotation and soil sustainability.
- **Otis Boykin** – Developed improved electrical resistors used in pacemakers and computers.
- **Kizzmekia Corbett** – Lead scientist in the development of the Moderna COVID-19 vaccine.

## 🌍 Impact & Legacy

- **Paved the way for diversity in STEM** and inspired future generations.
- **Advanced technological innovation** across multiple industries, from agriculture to artificial intelligence.
- **Expanded Black representation in tech leadership** and research fields.
- **Continues to drive discussions on equity in technology** and closing the digital divide.

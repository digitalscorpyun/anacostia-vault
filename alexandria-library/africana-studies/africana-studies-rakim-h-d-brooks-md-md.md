## Overview
Rakim H.D. Brooks is a civil rights lawyer and advocate, currently serving as the President of the **Alliance for Justice (AFJ)** as of 2025. With a background in law and policy reform, Brooks has been instrumental in advancing progressive judicial appointments and advocating for marginalized communities.

## ✊🏿 **Overview: Africana Studies – The Pulse of Black Resilience**

**Africana Studies** is a **radical interdisciplinary field** that unearths the **histories, cultures, and struggles** of African peoples across the diaspora. It’s not just scholarship—it’s a **weapon of resistance**, dismantling Eurocentric lies and amplifying **Black voices** through history, sociology, politics, philosophy, literature, and art. From ancient kingdoms to modern movements, it’s the ** heartbeat of Black identity and liberation**.

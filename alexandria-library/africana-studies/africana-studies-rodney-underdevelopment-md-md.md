# Rodney Underdevelopment  

**Summary**: Refers to Walter Rodney’s *How Europe Underdeveloped Africa*—argues European colonialism and capitalism systematically underdeveloped Africa through resource extraction and slavery, not inherent "primitivism."  

**Details**: Europe’s exploitation (e.g., Middle Passage, colonial trade) enriched itself while stunting Africa’s economy. Challenges Eurocentric myths, linking racial capitalism to modern inequities—key for reparations and decolonization.

If you were born in 1973, you are part of **Generation X** (commonly abbreviated as Gen X). This generation typically includes individuals born between **1965 and 1980**.

### **Characteristics of Generation X**:

- **Independent and Resourceful**: Often referred to as the "latchkey kids," Gen X grew up with greater independence and self-reliance.
- **Tech Transition Generation**: Experienced the shift from analog to digital, adapting to personal computers, the internet, and mobile phones.
- **Cultural Influences**: Shaped by 1980s and 1990s pop culture, including MTV, grunge music, and iconic films like _The Breakfast Club_.
- **Work Ethic**: Known for their pragmatic approach to work and life, often balancing skepticism with a strong work ethic.
- **Global Events**: Influenced by events like the end of the Cold War, the rise of globalization, and early environmental movements.

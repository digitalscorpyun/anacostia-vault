## 🎯 **What is CodeWars?**

CodeWars is a **community-driven platform** that trains developers through **kata** (short coding exercises). Your **goal** is to advance from **8 kyū** to **1 kyū**, eventually reaching **dan ranks (master level).** Along the way, you’ll **sharpen problem-solving skills, improve Python proficiency, and learn from others.**

---

## ⚔️ **Key Concepts**

### 🔹 **Kata**

Kata are coding challenges that test and develop your skills. Each kata is **ranked** based on difficulty. Once you solve a kata, you’ll be able to see **how others solved it**, which can introduce new techniques and optimizations.

### 🔹 **Ranks (Kyū & Dan System)**

The **kyū** system (borrowed from Japanese martial arts) ranks difficulty:

- **8 kyū (beginner) → 1 kyū (advanced)**
- After 1 kyū, you enter **dan ranks (master levels)**
- Harder kata give **more progress toward leveling up**

### 🔹 **Honor**

Honor points reflect **your contributions and engagement** on the site (solving katas, commenting, creating content). It’s **separate from rank** but shows **experience and involvement.**

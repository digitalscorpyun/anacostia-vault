# Project 2025: The Conservative Promise Overview
[[Project 2025 - Mandate For Leadership The Conservative Promise (Various Authors).epub]]

## Summary
*The Conservative Promise* outlines a policy agenda for the 2025 conservative transition, focusing on:
- **Restoring family values**
- **Dismantling the administrative state**
- **Defending national sovereignty**
- **Securing individual freedoms**

It draws parallels to the 1970s and Reagan's leadership, arguing that bold reforms are needed to counter progressive policies and global threats.

## Key Themes & Takeaways
- America is facing a crisis similar to the late 1970s.
- The book presents a roadmap inspired by Reagan-era policies.
- Four broad goals:
  1. Restore the family.
  2. Dismantle bureaucracy.
  3. Strengthen national sovereignty.
  4. Defend constitutional rights.

## Organizational Structure
- **Restoring the Family:** Policies to promote family stability and reduce government dependence.
- **Dismantling the Administrative State:** Reducing federal bureaucracy and restoring constitutional governance.
- **Defending National Sovereignty:** Securing borders, countering China, and revitalizing domestic industries.
- **Securing Individual Liberties:** Promoting free enterprise, educational reform, and cultural resilience.

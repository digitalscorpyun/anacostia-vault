# 🏛 **Africana Studies - Free Palestine Movement**

> _"Palestine is the cement that holds the Arab world together, or it is the explosive that blows it apart." — Yasser Arafat_

---

## 📌 **Overview**

The **Free Palestine Movement (FPM)** is a **Palestinian-Syrian organization** that began as a **community and political advocacy group** but later transitioned into an **armed militia aligned with the Syrian Ba’athist government**. Founded by **Yasser Qashlaq**, a Palestinian-Syrian businessman, the FPM initially focused on **social services for Palestinians in Syria and Gaza**. However, after the outbreak of the **Syrian Civil War (2011)**, it formed a **military wing**, supporting **Bashar al-Assad's regime** and fighting against opposition forces.

After the **fall of the Assad government in 2024**, the **Syrian transitional government ordered the disbandment** of all Palestinian armed factions, including the **FPM**, forcing it to shift back into **political and social advocacy**.

---

## 🏷️ **Key Details**

- **Founded:** Early 2000s by **Yasser Qashlaq**.
- **Initial Purpose:** Community and **social services for Palestinian refugees** in Syria.
- **Political Alignment:** **Pro-Ba'athist, Pro-Assad**, opposed to Israel.
- **Military Activity:** Engaged in the **Syrian Civil War**, fighting rebels and ISIS.
- **Current Status (Post-2024):** **Disarmed** and ordered to shift focus to **non-military political work**.

## 📜 **Historical Timeline**

📍 **2003-2011** – FPM provides **social services** to Palestinians in Syria and supports activism in **Gaza**.  
📍 **2011-2024** – Syrian Civil War begins; FPM forms its **armed wing** and fights alongside **Assad’s forces**.  
📍 **2018** – FPM’s military commander, **Saed Abd Al-Aal**, is **wounded in battle**.  
📍 **2024** – Assad falls; **FPM ordered to disarm** by the **new Syrian government**.  
📍 **Present** – The organization now operates as a **political and charitable group**.

---

## ⚖️ **Political & Military Activities**

- Originally an **advocacy and social service organization** for **Palestinians in Syria and Gaza**.
- Became **militarized in 2011**, forming the **Al-Aqsa Shield Forces** to fight against **Syrian rebels**.
- **Fought ISIS** and **opposition groups** in **Damascus, Deir ez-Zor, and Idlib**.
- By **2024**, following the fall of Assad, FPM was **forced to demobilize**.

## 🌟 **Impact & Legacy**

- The **FPM’s role in Syria** blurred the lines between **Palestinian liberation and regional conflicts**.
- **"Free Palestine"** became a **global slogan**, often unrelated to the **FPM’s military activities**.
- Accusations of **war crimes and forced displacement** still **haunt** former FPM members.

## ⚠️ **Controversies & Allegations**

- **War Crimes:** Accused of **targeting civilians** during the **Siege of Yarmouk Camp**.
- **Criminal Activities:** Forced **land sales and financial corruption**.
- **Human Rights Abuses:** Former member **Mofeq AlDaouah was convicted of war crimes in Germany**.

### Summary: Inside Leonard Leo’s Teneo Network – A Conservative Powerhouse

**Overview**:  
Leonard Leo, a central figure in the Supreme Court's conservative supermajority, has taken leadership of the **Teneo Network**, a private, "confidential" group aiming to counter liberal influence across American politics, culture, and institutions. Teneo, modeled after the [[Federalist Society]], seeks to create networks of conservative leaders in fields such as finance, media, and education.

---

### **Key Points**:

#### **Origins and Leadership**:

- **Founded in 2008** by Evan Baehr, Josh Hawley, and others, Teneo initially struggled financially but was revamped under Leo’s guidance starting in 2021.
- Leo’s leadership, coupled with significant financial backing, transformed Teneo into a thriving organization, with **nearly $5 million in revenue by 2021**, including major contributions from **DonorsTrust**, Paul Singer, and the DeVos family.

#### **Mission and Vision**:

- Teneo aims to create a “**Silicon Valley of Conservatism**,” connecting young conservative leaders to promote a worldview centered on limited government, free enterprise, and traditional values.
- Activities include helping members secure influential roles, funding startups, and advancing conservative causes in media, academia, and corporate America.

#### **Membership and Influence**:

- Members include prominent conservatives like Senators **Josh Hawley** and **J.D. Vance**, Representative **Elise Stefanik**, Florida Governor **Ron DeSantis’ aides**, and media figure **Ben Shapiro**.
- Teneo provides access to a **powerful network** of allies in politics, business, and law, offering resources for members’ personal and professional growth.

#### **Strategic Initiatives**:

- Teneo works on projects like countering "woke capitalism" and mapping key institutions in major cities to embed conservative leaders.
- Annual retreats host prominent figures such as **Ted Cruz**, **Vivek Ramaswamy**, and **Nikki Haley**, facilitating networking and collaboration.

#### **Tactics and Secrecy**:

- Teneo emphasizes confidentiality, with a discreet website and policies to shield members’ affiliations from public scrutiny.
- Members are involved in shaping state-level policies, launching investigations, and driving opposition to environmental, social, and governance (ESG) policies in corporations.

#### **Future Goals**:

- Teneo envisions a long-term cultural transformation, fostering leadership to influence politics, education, and media.
- It aims to build networks that solidify conservative influence for decades, with Leo emphasizing trust and fellowship as foundational to their mission.

---

### **Significance**:

Teneo represents a strategic shift in conservative organizing, expanding beyond judicial and legislative battles to broader cultural and institutional arenas. Its secrecy, influential membership, and deep financial backing position it as a significant force in shaping American politics and society.

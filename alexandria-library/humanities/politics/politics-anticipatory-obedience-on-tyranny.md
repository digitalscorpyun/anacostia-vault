# Anticipatory Obedience – On Tyranny  
## 🔍 Definition  
Anticipatory obedience occurs when individuals conform to perceived power structures before being forced, strengthening authoritarian rule.

## 📜 Historical Examples  
- **Nazi Germany (1930s)** – Citizens and bureaucrats implemented policies **before** legal enforcement.
- **Czechoslovakia (1946-48)** – People aligned with communist rule preemptively, easing the transition to a one-party state.

## 🎯 Relevance Today  
- People self-censor in authoritarian environments, **teaching power what it can get away with**.
- Tech companies, fearing regulation, may voluntarily restrict speech before being forced.

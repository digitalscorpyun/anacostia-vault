## **📜 Overview**

Movement for a People’s Party (MPP) is a campaign led by former campaign staffers for the **2016 Presidential campaign** of self-described **“democratic socialist”** U.S. Senator **Bernie Sanders** (I-Vermont). The movement seeks to establish a **progressive political party** to challenge the **Democratic Party** from the left. MPP aims to **build coalitions** and establish **state groups** that will **supplant the Democratic Party** and implement a **far-left platform**, including **government welfare programs** and **increased economic regulations**. #BernieSanders #ProgressiveMovement

### **📌 Policy Positions** #PolicyAgenda #SocialJustice

✅ Government-run **single-payer healthcare** 🏥 #HealthcareReform  
✅ Taxpayer-funded **public college tuition** 🎓 #EducationForAll

✅ **Protected access to abortion rights** ⚖️ #ReproductiveRights

## **📖 History and Organization**

Founded in **2017** by **Nick Brana**, the organization was originally called **Draft Bernie for a People’s Party**. It is a project of the non-profit **People for a Working Democracy**, which **does not have publicly available tax filings**. #PoliticalHistory #PeopleForADemocracy

## **⚡ Activities** #Activism #GrassrootsMovement

MPP claims to be **building a far-left coalition** capable of supplanting the **Democratic Party** on a **state-by-state basis**, leading to a **“political revolution”**. The organization has partnered with **labor, environmental, and anti-war groups** in **protests and boycotts**.

### **📅 Key Events** #PoliticalEvents

- **🗓️ September 23, 2019:** Participated in **Shut Down DC climate protests**, advocating against **capitalism** in **environmental policy**. 🌱✊ #ClimateAction #AntiCapitalism

## **👥 Leadership** #Leadership

**Nick Brana** is the **Founder and Executive Director**. He previously worked as the **national political outreach coordinator for Bernie Sanders’ 2016 campaign**. He left the **Democratic Party** in **2017**, citing **corruption and corporate influence**. #PoliticalStrategy

### **🗳️ 2020 Presidential Cycle** #Elections

In **December 2018**, Brana was invited to join Bernie Sanders’ **2020 Presidential campaign** but **declined**, alleging that the **Democratic National Committee (DNC) rigged the primary process** against **Sanders**. #ElectionIntegrity

## **⚠️ Opposition to Nuclear Energy** #EnergyPolicy #EnvironmentalActivism

In **May 2021**, MPP **co-signed a letter** to **Congress** opposing **nuclear energy**, calling it **“dirty”** and a **“significant” source of pollution**. The letter advocated for a **renewable electricity standard** excluding **nuclear and natural gas**. #RenewableEnergy

### **⚡ Nuclear Energy Facts** #ClimateFacts

- **⚛️ Nuclear plants produce no carbon dioxide** and accounted for **19% of U.S. electricity production in 2021**.
- The **Nature Conservancy (2018)** recommended increasing nuclear capacity to **33% of total world energy output** to reduce carbon emissions. 🌱 #ClimateSolutions

Proverbs 18:10 (Amplified Bible - AMP) states:

**"The name of the Lord is a strong tower;  
The righteous runs to it and is safe and set on high far above evil."**

### **Explanation:**

- **"The name of the Lord"** refers to God's character, power, and authority, emphasizing His ability to protect and provide refuge.
- **"A strong tower"** symbolizes security, strength, and an unshakable place of safety.
- **"The righteous runs to it"** implies that those who trust in God seek Him in times of trouble and rely on His protection.
- **"Safe and set on high"** indicates not only protection but also elevation above harm and danger, suggesting spiritual security and divine oversight.

This verse encourages believers to find strength and refuge in God, highlighting faith as a source of ultimate security and protection from life's challenges.

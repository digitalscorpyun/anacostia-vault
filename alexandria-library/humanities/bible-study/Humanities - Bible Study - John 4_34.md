#Holy_Bible #New_Testament #spritual_nourishment 
**John 4:34 (Amplified Bible)**:

**"Jesus said to them, ‘My food is to do the will of Him who sent Me and to completely finish His work.’"**

---

### **Context and Meaning**:

- In this verse, Jesus speaks to His disciples after His encounter with the Samaritan woman at the well. They had urged Him to eat, but He responds by emphasizing His spiritual mission over physical nourishment.
- **"My food"**: This metaphor highlights that Jesus finds sustenance and fulfillment in doing God's will.
- **"The will of Him who sent Me"**: Refers to God the Father and the divine purpose of salvation and reconciliation.
- **"To completely finish His work"**: Indicates Jesus' commitment to fulfilling God's redemptive plan, which ultimately leads to the cross.

This verse reminds believers of the importance of prioritizing spiritual purposes and aligning their lives with God's mission.

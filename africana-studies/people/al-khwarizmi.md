
# **Al-Khwarizmi**

> _"The study of mathematics, like the Nile, begins in minuteness but ends in magnificence."_ – Al-Khwarizmi

## 📌 Overview

**Al-Khwarizmi** is a pivotal figure in **Africana Studies & Mathematics**, recognized as the "father of algebra." His work on algorithms and numerical systems forms the foundation of modern computing, making his contributions essential to both historical analysis and technological advancements. His influence extends to AI, cryptography, and data science, aligning with _The Lion of Anacostia_ project for bias detection in Africana Studies.
